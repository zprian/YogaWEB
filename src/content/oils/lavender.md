Lavender (*Lavandula angustifolia*) is often called the "mother of all essential oils" due to its incredible versatility. In a yoga practice, diffusing lavender during Savasana (final relaxation) creates a deeply peaceful atmosphere that helps the nervous system fully unwind.

**How to use:** Diffuse 3–4 drops during restorative or yin yoga. Apply diluted to temples or wrists before meditation.
