Eucalyptus (*Eucalyptus globulus*) is a powerhouse oil for yoga practitioners. Its refreshing, camphoraceous scent opens the airways almost instantly, supporting the pranayama (breathwork) practices central to yoga.

**How to use:** Diffuse 2–3 drops during morning or power yoga classes. Add a drop to a bowl of hot water for steam inhalation before practice.
