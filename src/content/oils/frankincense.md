Frankincense (*Boswellia sacra*) has been used in spiritual and meditative practices for thousands of years. Its rich, warm, resinous aroma has a profound grounding effect on the mind and nervous system, making it the ideal companion for meditation and deeper yoga practices.

**How to use:** Diffuse during meditation or slow-flow yoga. Apply one drop to the crown of the head or third-eye point before practice.
